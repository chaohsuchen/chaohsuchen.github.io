MUN - A Turning Point

I was like most of the students—being an introvert. However, during the journey of growing, I found that this characteristic deter me from making friends and grabbing the opportunity to pursue a better self. Determined, I joined MUN and become the delegate of France. Gradually, I built up my courage and became a person who love to socialize with others, and, fortunately, I was credited with the best diplomacy.

從前，我如多數的孩子一樣，是一位內向的孩子。但是，我逐漸發覺這種個性使我不容易交到朋友，甚至也因此錯失了許多能精進自己的機會。於是，當時的我下定決心，參加了模擬聯合國，擔任了法國代表。在一進一退之中，我學會應對進退的技巧，甚至愛上了交新朋友。最後，我很幸運地榮獲最佳外交獎，這項殊榮也成為我日後擁有外向性格的墊腳石。

Because of MUN, I had built up the wisdom to speak and interact with new friends without making the coversation being awkward and bizarre. This truly benefits me especially after I entered college.



因為參加了模擬聯合國，我建立起了與人們交談時的態度與方法，使得在與新朋友交談時，不會讓話題變得尷尬又無趣。這更幫助我在日後大學時快速地結交各系的朋友。

CS - Jounery of Exploration

There are always myths lingering over Computer Science, which has been stamped them as the department of coding. Hence, before I entered college, I was not sure about whether I would like this major. Wondered and unsecured, I entered CS a year ago, which redefined my characteristic thoroughly. I attended a miscellaneous of courses in our department, which endows me the opportunity to savor the essence and beauty of Computer Science. I realized it is not only about coding, it focusses more on training us to have the ability to create and materialize our ideas.

當提起資工系，多數人會認為資工系的學生只是在學程式，而我也曾因為這項原因，帶著懷疑與不確定進入了資工系。然而，我逐漸意識到資工系其實教會我的不僅是撰寫程式，更教會如何將想法優化，並且將其實踐於電腦之中。

Having the ability to communicate with computer is always fascinating, and this is the reason which drives me to keep improving my skills, be it on Front End or Back End. I am looking forward to join tech companies in future, and immerse myself in the hope of vision and advancement, which can, will, and must be accomplish with the help of computer.

我一直以來認為能與電腦溝通是一件有趣且令人著迷的事，而這也是我學習的動力來源，不論是前端或是後端。我也期待未來能在科技公司當工程師，當一個有理想與抱負的人。

Future - Vision and Anticipation

I love English, especially after participated international camps and studied the materials of my major. I came to realize that English is like an intangible line, which connects all the human and knowledge around the globe tightly. I hope to keep improving my English ability.

我熱愛因英文，尤其是在參加國際營隊與修讀資工相關的內容後，我理解到英文如隱形的橋樑，連接了世界的知識與人們。我也期待未來能繼續精進英文。

I am eager to study abroad for my MS degree, for I always believe it would be a wonderful chance to acquaint different people around the world and immerse myself in a different educational environment.

我期望能到國外修讀碩士，因為我相信除了擁有高度競爭的學習換境外，也能結交各國的朋友，更能體驗不同的教育環境。
